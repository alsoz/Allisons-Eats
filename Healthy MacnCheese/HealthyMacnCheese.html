Jess' Healthy Mac 'n' Cheese

  • Yield: 2-3
  • Prep time: 25 minutes
  • Total time: 45

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Macaroni or chosen pasta
  • 1 cup butternut squash puree
  • 100ml evaporated milk
  • 4 tablespoons chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons low fat cream cheese
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 handfuls of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 medium onion, sliced thinly
  • 1 large apple, chopped or grated

Methods

  1. First cook the macaroni.
  2. Then fry the onion in butter in a saucepan, add the butternut squash, milk, salt, vegetable broth and give it a good stir.
  3. Next add in the grated cheese and grated apple.
  4. Drain the macaroni when cooked, and add the sauce.
  5. Mix well and enjoy!

Hints and tips

  • You could also tip the macaroni into a casserole of baking dish, pour over the sauce, sprinkle breadcrumbs and grated cheese over the top to add a delicious crispy topping.
  • You can use soy milk or lactose free milk and cheese for a lactose-free alternative.
  • This recipe can be made suitable for vegans by swapping milk for soy milk, cheese for vegan cheese and butter for vegan friendly spread, or just use oil.
