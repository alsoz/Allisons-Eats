Dark Chocolate and Almond Tart

Dark Chocolate and Almond Tart

yield: 8 servings prep time: 20 minutes total time: 3 hours

Ingredients

For Crust:

  • 240g ground almonds
  • pinch of salt
  • 3 Tbs melted coconut oil
  • 2 Tbs maple syrup
  • 1 egg, whisked

For Filling:

  • 250mL coconut cream
  • 200g dark chocolate (minimum 70%)
  • 35g coconut sugar
  • pinch of salt

For Topping:

  • 35g flaked almonds and/or toasted coconut

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Combine all crust ingredientsin a bowl and mix well. It should be crumbly.
  3. Grease a 20cm tart tin with a little coconut oil.
  4. Press crust mixture into tin and up the sides.
  5. Bake for about 15 minutes, until lightly golden.
  6. As crust is cooling, add all filling ingredients to a saucepan and head on medium-low while whisking.
  7. Whisk until melted and then pour into prepared crust.
  8. Cool for 1-2 hours in the fridge
  9. When the tart is set, decorate with coconut and almond flakes

Notes

  • This recipe is gluten-free, dairy-free, and paleo.
  • It works best with dark chocolate, but could perhaps work with milk chocolate. However this hasn't been tested.
  • Coconut milk could replace the cream, but cream gives it a richer texture.
