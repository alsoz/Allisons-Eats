Chocolate Lava Cakes

yield: 4 or 6 cakes prep time: 10 minutes total time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 170g milk, semi-sweet, or dark chocolate
  • 115g unsalted butter, plus extra for ramekins
  • 30g flour
  • 60g sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • 4 eggs
  • Cocoa powder, for dusting
  • Vanilla extract or vanilla bean paste, optional

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C.
  2. Coat 4 small ramekins (or 6 spots in a cupcake tin) with reserved butter. Coat with cocoa powder.
  3. Place butter and chocolate in a medium, microwave-safe bowl. Microwave in 30 second intervals until melted, mixing each time.
  4. Add in remaining ingredients until well mixed.
  5. Pour evenly into ramekins or cupcake tin. If using ramekins, place on a baking sheet.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 12-14 minutes.
  7. Let sit for 1 minute and then invert onto a plate. If using a cupcake tin, use a fork or spoon to remove them.
  8. Serve while warm with custard, ice cream, or any other ingredients.

Notes

  • Dark chocolate works the best for this recipe, but it depends on your personal preferences.
  • Make sure to coat ramekins very well so that the cakes come out clean.
  • Vanilla adds a little extra flavor to them, but it's entirely optional. You could also try adding in other flavours if you are feeling adventurous!
  • Try not baking them past 14 minutes. The center will be molten (hence the name).
